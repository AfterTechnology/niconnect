University system in Tanzania

The university system in Tanzania includes public and private institutions, with the University of Dar es Salaam being the oldest and largest. The Tanzania Commission for Universities (TCU) oversees higher education, ensuring quality and standards. Universities offer diverse programs in arts, sciences, technology, and professional fields, contributing to national development through education, research, and innovation.
